## Nami-s-Little-World

Introducing a simple, retro‑style web game inspired by my kitten, Nami. I built this project out of boredom and curiosity, turning it into a playful way to cure my boredom.

Nami is lively, cute, and full of energy — in this game, you’ll interact with her through simple actions like feeding, petting, and playing. Each choice changes her mood and brings her personality to life.

Please take care of her properly and enjoy the cozy.
